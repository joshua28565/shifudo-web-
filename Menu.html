Crispy skinned Black Sea Bass

Firm and lean with a mild delicate flavor profile, black sea bass has a tender, flaky texture best roasted, broiled or pan seared to get a crispy skin. Black Sea Bass is a good low-fat source of protein and magnesium. Magnesium is important for many processes in the body, including regulating muscle and nerve function, blood sugar levels, and blood pressure and making protein, bone, and DNA. Excellent for low carb paleo and keto diets.

INGREDIENTS:
Oil-packed anchovy fillets,
Small garlic clove thinly sliced, Chopped tender herbs (such as parsley, dill, and/or basil),Chopped pickles (capers, cornichons, or chile, Fresh lemon or lime juice or white wine vinegar, Extra-virgin olive oil, Divided
Kosher salt, Freshly ground pepper, Skin-on black bass, Striped bass, Snapper, or salmon fillets
Flaky sea salt.
